Instagram metadata

Instagram blocks metadata requests without a Meta app token. Getting one is free and takes about 5 minutes — anyone sharing this app can follow the same steps.

  1. Go to developers.facebook.com/apps and sign in with any Facebook account.
  2. Click Create App, choose Other, then Consumer, and give it any name.
  3. Once created, go to App Settings → Basic. Copy the App ID and App secret.
  4. Your token is: App ID|App secret (joined with a pipe character, no spaces).
  5. Paste it below and click Save.
